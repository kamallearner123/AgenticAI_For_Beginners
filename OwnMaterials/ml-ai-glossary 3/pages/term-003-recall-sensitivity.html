Recall (Sensitivity)

Among true positives, how many did we correctly identify.

Why it matters

Recall (Sensitivity) is commonly used in the Evaluation category. Understanding it helps you make better choices when designing pipelines, evaluating models, and communicating results.

Intuition

Among true positives, how many did we correctly identify.

Concrete example

Example: Of 60 spam emails, the filter caught 45 → recall = 45/60 = 0.75.

In practice

  • Where you’ll see it: evaluation tasks and tutorials.
